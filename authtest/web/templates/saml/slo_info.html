How Single Logout Works

SP-Initiated Logout

  1. SP sends LogoutRequest to IdP
  2. IdP terminates session
  3. IdP sends LogoutResponse to SP
  4. SP confirms logout

IdP-Initiated Logout

  1. IdP sends LogoutRequest to SP
  2. SP terminates local session
  3. SP sends LogoutResponse to IdP
  4. IdP continues to other SPs
